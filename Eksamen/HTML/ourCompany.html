About us

Self-ED is a company that has a goal to give evert child access to good quality education. With focus on providing education in a safe environment where the children and their parents feel that they are learning in a protected setting.

We focus on giving a platform to every child and especially girls as we at Self-ED know that through education the world can solve many issues developing countries are facing today.

Education is a basic human right that sadly not every child has today and we are setting out on a mission to end this injustice. Every child should have access to quality education so that he or she can achieve all their dreams and aspirations.
