The Product

Self-ED is a company that has a goal to give every child access to good quality education. With focus on providing education in a safe environment where the children and their parents feel that they are learning in a protected setting.

The app is mainly for populations in developing countries. Statistics from various non-profit organizations have shown that children in developing countries do not have access to quality education (Rueckert, 2019).

In addition, the app focuses on giving girls free and quality education. It is a well-known fact that girls/women do not get access to the same opportunities as boys/men in developing countries.

Therefore, we wish to implement Self-ED in developing countries to increase the number of girls educating themselves and gaining primary and secondary education (Paddison, 2015).



The situation is worse in certain places. An example is the northern part of Nigeria, where the net attendance rate is 53 %, while for females it is only approximately 47 %.

The main reasons for not attending are economic barriers, socio-cultural norms and practices that discourage attendance in formal education, especially for girls (Unicef, 2017).



Pilot

Self-ED has therefore decided that the project will be launched in Nigeria. It will be a pilot project in Nigeria and ensuing this it will be implemented in other countries as well. The solution will mainly focus on implementing an education platform that covers primary- and secondary education.

In addition, Self-ED focuses on making quality education accessible for girls. We believe that with the help of our platform, girls will be able to educate themselves without breaking any socio-cultural norms and practices.
